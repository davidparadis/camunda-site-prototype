Camunda, State of Agentic Orchestration and Automation 2026 (1,150 IT leaders)

Break through the automation ceiling.

AI is getting more capable every quarter. Enterprises are deploying agents faster than ever. But there's a widening gap between what AI can do and what enterprises actually get from it.

The end-to-end process still depends on manual handoffs, human exception handling, and stitched-together integrations. Each new bot or agent adds capability but also fragmentation. Results flatten. Costs rise. The automation ceiling holds.

What makes our agentic orchestration different

Four capabilities that no other vendor delivers together.

Outside & inside orchestration

We inject must-follow operating procedures between the agent's brain and its hands. If an agent decides to send an email, BPMN can force a human approval step first. No other vendor does this.

Deterministic + dynamic

Straight-through processing for predictable steps. Agent reasoning for exceptions. Seamlessly blended in one BPMN process, with the ability to dial autonomy up or down per step.

Agent-agnostic & composable

Orchestrate agents from any provider—Microsoft, LangChain, CrewAI, custom-built—via MCP and A2A protocol connectors. No lock-in. Change LLMs or runtimes without rewriting process logic.

Enterprise-grade trust

Zeebe handles millions of stateful process instances over weeks or months with full auditability. A mission-critical process engine with a decade of production hardening, now extended for agents.
